About IJETAE

(An Open Access ISO 9001:2008 Certified Journal)

The International Journal of Emerging Technology and Advanced Engineering

(ISSN 2250–2459(Online))
is online open access journal devoted to the publication of high quality papers on theoretical as well as practical developments in all engineering disciplines under the domain of Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering and Electrical Engineering. Original research papers, state-of-the-art reviews and technical notes are invited for publication.

IJETAE (ISSN 2250–2459 (Online)) is approved by National Science Library (NSL), National Institute of Science Communication And Information Resources (NISCAIR), Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, New Delhi, India.

IJETAE is Refereed International Journal publishing articles that emphasizes research, development and applications within the fields of new engineering and technology in the domain of Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering and Electrical Engineering. IJETAE is steered by a distinguished Board of Directors, Researchers and Academicians is supported by an international review board consisting of prominent individuals representing many well-known universities, colleges, and corporate world.
